Identified problems
1. Lack of appreciation for little achievements

Playing around with psychology
Based on reading certain articles from top UX researches, product designers and managers, we tried to play around some word physchology.
Once the user accepts any challenge to do, we displayed a small dialog box asking for a deadline within which the user expects himself to complete it.
Also we changed the word accepted to pending inorder to create an urgency in the mind of the users.
